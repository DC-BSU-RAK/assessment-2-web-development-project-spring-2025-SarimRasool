About Subway Surfers

Subway Surfers is an endless runner mobile game co-developed by Kiloo and SYBO Games, private companies based in Denmark. It is available on Android, iOS, Kindle, and Windows Phone platforms.

The game starts with the player character running along subway train tracks, dodging incoming trains and other obstacles. The player can swipe up, down, left, or right to avoid obstacles and collect coins and power-ups.

Since its release in 2012, Subway Surfers has become one of the most downloaded mobile games worldwide, with over 3 billion downloads as of 2022.
